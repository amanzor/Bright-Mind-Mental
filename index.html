About Anna

A practice grounded in presence, not pressure.

Anna Rondon is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor and National Certified Counselor based in the Miami area, with telehealth services serving clients across the state of Florida. Her practice is built on a simple conviction: real change rarely arrives in a single leap. It comes in small, deliberate steps — taken with someone who sees you clearly and walks alongside you.

With clinical experience spanning forensic psychology and juvenile services, Anna brings a deep understanding of diverse behavioral and emotional challenges. She is especially passionate about working with adolescents, while also providing therapeutic support to children, adults, and families navigating high-risk seasons of life.

The Approach

Small steps. Real ground.

  • We meet

    An introductory conversation to understand what brought you here and what you're hoping for. Fit matters — and there's no pressure to continue.

  • We listen, fully

    Before any framework or technique, the work begins with being heard. Without rush. Without a script. Without judgment.

  • We map a path

    Together, we set goals that feel honest and reachable — drawing from evidence-based modalities including CBT, DBT, Functional Family Therapy, Systems Theory, attachment-based work, and trauma-informed care.

  • We walk it

    Therapy isn't a destination — it's a steady practice of returning to yourself, building insight, resilience, and emotional well-being. One baby step at a time.
