Frequently Asked Questions

Therapy is a collaborative, confidential process where you work with a licensed counselor to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Through evidence-based techniques and a supportive relationship, we help you develop coping strategies, gain clarity, and move toward meaningful change. Sessions are typically 50-60 minutes and tailored to your unique needs and goals.

The length of therapy varies based on your specific concerns and goals. Some people benefit from short-term therapy (5-10 sessions) for focused issues, while others may engage in longer-term therapy (months to years) for deeper work or ongoing support. We'll discuss your goals during your first session and adjust the timeline as needed.

Yes! We offer both in-person and telehealth therapy sessions. Our telehealth services are available throughout Florida, providing the same quality care from the comfort of your home. You can choose the format that works best for you, and we can discuss whether in-person or virtual sessions are most suitable for your needs.

Our approach is grounded in presence and evidence-based practice. We integrate cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), behavioral therapy, and other proven techniques to help you understand patterns and develop practical strategies. Above all, we believe in the power of the therapeutic relationship—a safe, non-judgmental space where real change can happen.

Getting started is easy! You can reach out via phone at (954) 282-9668 or email anna@brightmindmental.com. You can also book directly through our booking page. We'll discuss your needs, answer any questions, and schedule your first session at a time that works for you. There's no pressure—just a conversation to see if we're a good fit.

Yes. Everything you share in therapy is confidential and protected by law. There are only a few exceptions—such as if you're in immediate danger, there's abuse of a child or vulnerable adult, or a court orders us to disclose information. We'll discuss these limits upfront so you know exactly what to expect.

We're happy to work with you on payment options. Please contact us to discuss insurance coverage or sliding scale rates. Our goal is to make therapy accessible, and we can explore options that fit your financial situation.
